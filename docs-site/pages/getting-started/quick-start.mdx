# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Pariflow! This guide will help you get started with the platform quickly.

## What is Pariflow?

Pariflow is a production-grade prediction market platform where users trade on future outcomes. The platform combines:

- **Hybrid Trading**: AMM (Automated Market Maker) + Order Book
- **Real-time Updates**: WebSocket-powered live prices
- **Risk Management**: Automated hedging with Polymarket
- **Sports Integration**: Real-time sports betting markets

## For Users

### 1. Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit [pariflow.com](https://pariflow.com)
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Select your wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
4. Approve the connection

### 2. Deposit Funds

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Deposit"
3. Follow the instructions to deposit USDC or other supported tokens
4. Wait for confirmation (usually 1-2 minutes)

### 3. Place Your First Trade

1. Browse available markets on the homepage
2. Click on an event you're interested in
3. Choose your position (YES or NO)
4. Enter the amount you want to trade
5. Select order type:
   - **Market Order**: Execute immediately at current price
   - **Limit Order**: Set your desired price
6. Click "Place Order"

### 4. Monitor Your Positions

- View your active positions in the "Portfolio" section
- Track your PnL (Profit and Loss) in real-time
- Set up notifications for important market events

### 2. Authenticate

```bash
curl -X GET https://api.pariflow.com/api/institutional/market-data/trades \
  -H "X-API-Key: your-api-key-here" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json"
```
